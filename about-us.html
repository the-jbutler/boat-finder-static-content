Who is myboat.guru for?

We are here to help new boat buyers find the boat they will love, at a price they can afford. Without having to know your luff from your leech

Why are we here?

Because most people find out what kind of boat they really wanted AFTER they have bought their first boat. For example, boat salesman love to show you the lovely wide beds, and spacious hulls - they don't tell you that the extra weight makes the boat as slow as a pig.

They love to show you videos of the boat doing 20 knots - they don’t tell you that it will never do that once you have loaded up with fuel, water and groceries. They love to show you the enormous fridge full of beer - they don’t tell you that it will chew power you don't have.

3 Easy Steps

  1. You tell us about you
  2. Then we eliminate all the boats that are NOT right for you. Like that sleek French racer that can do 20 knots on a sneeze…until you load it with a few people, bottles of wine and groceries, when it wallows like a hippo
  3. Then we show you how to fit the boats you want to your budget

What is different about us? Two things…

  1. We have done all the donkey work. We have gone through every mainstream catamaran, painstakingly measured them and rated them for key fields like "will it be easy to sell when you are done" and "how comfortable will it be on an overnight passage". Real questions that real people need answering
  2. We only ask you questions that first time buyers can answer. For example "Is this a boat you will want to sell in a few years, or is this a boat for life" is much easier to answer than "Would you like a balsa wood core or a resin infused foam core"
However we never share any information without you specifically authorising us to (no sneaky data permission statements here) and we only share your information with brokers we trust who can do the job for you. Not because we are saints, but because that is how we make money.

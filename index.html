Are We Right For You?

Buying a boat? Frustrated? You are in the right place

One of the greatest myths of boating is that you can have any 2 of comfort, speed and price.

All the learned experts that tell you solemnly that you have to make sacrifices and compromises are right some of the time ‐ but not all of the time.

There are boats that combine great performance and comfort at a sensible price. And there are ways of stretching your budget, shrinking the cost

To find your way to the right solution for you, we have done all the donkey work. We have gone through every mainstream catamaran, painstakingly measured them and rated them for key fields like "will it be easy to sell when you are done" and "how comfortable will it be on an overnight passage". Real questions that real people need answering

All you need to do is…

3 Easy Steps

  1. You tell us about you
  2. Then we eliminate all the boats that are NOT right for you. Like that sleek French racer that can do 20 knots on a sneeze…until you load it with a few people, bottles of wine and groceries, when it wallows like a hippo
  3. Then we show you how to fit the boats you want to your budget

Are we biased? Are we for real? How do we make money?

Everyone is biased in some way (we all own boats and obviously think we made brilliant choices!)

But when it comes to making good recommendations to you, we make the most money by giving you the right recommendations.

We make money because people who want to meet you will pay you for the contact.

However we never share any information without you specifically authorising us to (no sneaky data permission statements here) and we only share your information with brokers we trust who can do the job for you. Not because we are saints, but because that is how we make money.
